About Mangalore

Mangalore, officially known as Mangaluru, is a major port city in the Indian state of Karnataka. It is known for its diverse culture, beautiful beaches, and delicious seafood. The city is also home to several historical landmarks and temples.

Getting There

Mangalore is well-connected by air, rail, and road. The Mangalore International Airport offers flights to major cities in India and abroad. The city is also accessible by train from various parts of the country, and there are regular bus services as well.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Mangalore is during the winter months from November to February when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing and beach activities. The monsoon season from June to September also offers a unique experience with lush greenery and waterfalls.

Top Attractions in Mangalore

Panambur Beach

One of the most popular beaches in Mangalore, known for its stunning sunsets, boat rides, and well-maintained clean shores. Perfect for evening walks and family outings.

St. Aloysius Chapel

A historic chapel known for its stunning frescoes and paintings. The interior walls feature the work of Italian artist Antonio Moscheni, making it a must-visit architectural marvel.

Kudroli Gokarnath Temple

A beautiful temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its stunning architecture and peaceful atmosphere. The temple is especially vibrant during Navaratri celebrations.

Sultan Battery

A historic watchtower built in black stones by Tipu Sultan. This 18th-century structure offers panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and the Gurpur River.

Mangaladevi Temple

The historic temple after which the city is named. This ancient temple is dedicated to Goddess Mangaladevi and showcases beautiful traditional architecture.

Tannirbhavi Beach

A serene and less crowded beach, ideal for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle. The beach is known for its clean sands and tranquil environment.
